Recycle

Recycle: verb - Convert (waste) into reusable material

Recycling tips

  • When shopping look for products that can be easily recycled and products that contain recycled content.
  • Sort your home waste and put it in your curbside recycle bins.
  • Many states have plastic bottle deposits so take them them back and get your deposit instead throwing them away.
  • Have used motor oil, many auto parts stores accepted used oil.
  • Left over paint can be recycled at many major hardware stores or at recycling centers.
  • Used batteries can also be taken to recycling centers or stores like Batteries Plus.
  • Most garages are required to accept and recycle your used tires when you have new ones installed.

Don't have bottle deposits or curb side pick up? Call around or Google "recycling centers near me". A list and a map will show you where to go.
